# DIVERSION

♻️ ScrapChain
Building the “Amazon of Waste” – Digitizing India’s Reverse Logistics Ecosism
📌 Overview

ScrapChain is a digital reverse-logistics marketplace designed to professionalize India’s highly informal scrap and e-waste ecosystem.

In India, over 60–90% of recycling is handled by informal kabadi-walas (scrap dealers). There is:

❌ No price transparency

❌ No standardized weighing

❌ No traceability of waste flow

❌ No digital integration with government compliance systems

ScrapChain solves this by creating a three-sided marketplace connecting:

🏠 Citizens

♻️ Kabadi-walas (Scrap Dealers)

🏭 Industrial Recyclers

By 2026, India’s e-waste market alone is projected to reach $2.02B. ScrapChain positions itself as the digital infrastructure layer for this growing industry.

🚀 Problem Statement

The recycling chain is fragmented and informal.

Prices fluctuate by locality.

Citizens don’t know if they are getting fair value.

Waste traceability ends at pickup.

Government struggles to meet E-Waste (Management) Rules 2023 and Swachh Bharat 2.0 targets.

💡 Solution

ScrapChain is a mobile-first digital aggregator platform focused on:

📊 Price Transparency

⚖️ Verifiable Digital Weighing

🔄 Reverse Logistics Optimization

📜 EPR Compliance Automation

📈 Waste Traceability

💰 Revenue Model – “The Material Spread”

ScrapChain earns from the value-added chain:

1️⃣ Transaction Margin

Buy from households at ₹X

Sell aggregated scrap at ₹X + 15%

Premium pricing for clean, sorted, high-volume scrap

2️⃣ EPR Credit Trading

Sell verified recycling credits to manufacturers

High-margin compliance revenue

3️⃣ SaaS for Scrap Dealers

Subscription-based Business Pro tools:

Digital scale integration

Inventory management

Logistics routing

Analytics dashboard

4️⃣ Data Insights

Sell anonymized consumption insights to FMCG brands

Example: Neighborhood plastic usage analytics
