Digital Weighing

The kabadi-wala uses a Bluetooth-integrated digital scale. Weight is synced to the app in real time, and UPI payment is triggered automatically — zero haggling, zero cheating.

03
🏭

Industrial Sale

Aggregated, sorted, and certified scrap volumes are sold directly to industrial recyclers at premium rates, with automatic EPR certificate generation for compliance.

The Hub & Spoke
Playbook

Phase 01
Q1–Q2 2025
The Pilot Ward

Master one high-density urban ward. Onboard 50 kabadi-walas and 5,000 households. Prove price transparency, digital weighing, and UPI payment work at ground level.

50 Kabadi-walas 5K Households UPI Integration
Phase 02
Q3–Q4 2025
The Micro-Hub

Establish a Micro-Sorting Centre where kabadi-walas deliver daily collections. Deploy semi-automated baling machines. Begin selling certified, sorted tons directly to industrial buyers.

Sorting Centre Industrial Sales EPR Credits
Phase 03
2026
The Franchise Model

License the ScrapChain brand and SaaS platform to existing large-scale scrap dealers in Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities. Zero capex expansion. Revenue via SaaS subscriptions and transaction margins.

SaaS Licensing Tier-2 Cities City Partnerships
Phase 04
2027+
The Circular Marketplace

Launch an e-commerce section for upcycled products — furniture from wood waste, plastic road bricks, art from e-waste. Close the loop from discarded to desired.
